Introduction

Ways of thinking, problem solving and creating in Computer Science have become invaluable parts of life and are important beyond ensuring that we have enough skilled workers. Computer Science as a discipline incorporates more than just coding, it involves physical systems and networks, the collection, storage and analysis of data and the impact of computing on society!
